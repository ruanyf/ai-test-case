FAQ

Questions, answered

Can’t find what you’re looking for? Contact our team.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You can keep using the Starter plan for free with up to 15 users.

Can we migrate data from another tool?

Yes. CSV imports are supported for projects, tasks, and team members. Enterprise plans include white-glove migration assistance.

Does SkillSync include onboarding help?

Growth and Enterprise plans include guided onboarding sessions. We also have a library of self-serve guides and video walkthroughs.

Is there an annual contract?

No contract is required. Annual billing offers a 20% discount and you can switch or cancel at any time.

How does SkillSync handle security?

SkillSync is SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR-ready, and offers SSO, audit logs, and role-based permissions on Enterprise plans.
