About SkillSync

Built by a distributed team that was tired of switching between six different apps just to get one project done.

SkillSync started as a small internal system and grew into the product teams use today to keep work connected. We added shared docs, real-time updates, and integrations so departments can manage more of their daily workflow in one place.

Today, thousands of teams rely on SkillSync when they need a tool that is powerful enough to organize complex work and simple enough for everyone to adopt in days, not months.

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You can invite your whole team and test every feature before paying.

Can we migrate data from another tool?

Yes. CSV imports are supported for projects, tasks, and team members. Growth and Enterprise plans also include assisted migration.

Does SkillSync include onboarding help?

Growth and Enterprise plans include guided onboarding sessions. Starter customers have access to our help center and email support.

Is there an annual contract?

No contract is required. You can pay month-to-month, but annual billing offers a discount for teams that want to commit.
