About SkillSync

SkillSync started as a small internal system and then it slowly became a product we decided to continue working on.

After some time we added docs, updates, and a few integrations so teams can do more of their daily workflow in one tool.

Now different departments use it when they do not want to set up something too complicated.

FAQ Stuff

Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

Yes. CSV imports are supported for projects, tasks, and team members.

Growth and Enterprise plans include guided onboarding sessions.
